Review SGSI-POLICY-001 key sections\n5. Collect signature (DocuSign or physical)\n6. Upload signed PDF to `docs/02-policies/information-security-policy-SIGNED.pdf`\n7. Email policy to all staff\n8. Mark as complete in GAP-006\n**Critical**: Without signature = automatic audit failure\n**ETA**: CEO 30min, prep 1h
